## Visual Rules
- Use `Cormorant Garamond` for expressive editorial headers.
- Use `Inter` for compact utility labels and captions.
- Use `Figma Hand` for handwritten notes and diary snippets.
- Prefer layered paper, tape, torn edges, and collage density over flat minimal layouts.
- Keep pages full and lively, but avoid loud primaries or harsh red accents.
- Core palette: oat, paper white, cocoa, warm gray, sage, blush.
